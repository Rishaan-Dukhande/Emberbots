Community Impact
Outreach Programs

We believe robotics education should reach every student. Our team runs multiple programs to bring STEAM to our community.

🔦

Torchbearing Tutors

A division of FHS Robotics dedicated to teaching young children about STEAM through hands-on activities and mentorship sessions.

🧪

Kindling Kits

In-home lab kits and experiments provided to students, bringing science and engineering discovery directly into their homes.

🤖

FLL Demo Events

We showcased our robot at a local FLL tournament — 250+ attendees, robot drive time, and nearly 80% of students eager to pursue FTC.

🚁

Matternet Workshop

Our team attended a private event hosted by Matternet, an autonomous drone delivery company. We learned how real-world innovation is applied to engineering and brought those lessons back into our own design process.
