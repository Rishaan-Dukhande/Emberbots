About the Emberbots

We are the Emberbots, a FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team from Fremont High School. Since our founding in 2019, our mission has been to spark curiosity and passion for STEM in young people through competitive robotics, hands-on learning, and community engagement.

Our team is made up of dedicated student engineers, programmers, and strategists who collaborate across disciplines to design, build, and compete with our robot each season. We believe that every student has the potential to be an innovator.
